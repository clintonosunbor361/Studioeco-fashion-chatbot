# Studio Eco — Design System

## Brand
Name: Eco by Studio Eco
Tagline: Your smartest team member
Positioning: AI-powered chatbot that handles operations, answers questions, and keeps your business moving

## Colors
--bg: #f4f3ee (warm off-white — primary background)
--surface: #e9e7df (slightly richer warm grey — cards, panels)
--ink: #0f0e0c (near-black — primary text)
--ink-muted: #5b5751 (muted warm brown — secondary text, labels)
--accent: #1a4d2e (deep forest green — primary accent, CTAs)
--accent-light: #dde8de (soft sage — hover states, backgrounds)
--rule: rgba(15,14,12,0.12) (near-invisible dividers)

## Typography
Primary font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Display headers: Inter 700, tight letter spacing (-0.03em), large scale
- Body: Inter 400, 16-18px, line-height 1.6
- Labels/UI: Inter 500, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.1em, small size 11-12px
- No serif fonts anywhere

## Spacing
Base unit: 4px
Scale: 8, 16, 24, 32, 48, 64, 96, 128px
Section padding: clamp(6rem, 12vw, 12rem) top and bottom

## Components
Buttons:
- Primary: background #1a4d2e, white text, border-radius 999px (pill), padding 14px 28px
- Secondary: transparent background, #0f0e0c border 1px, border-radius 999px
- Both carry a small arrow icon on the right

Cards:
- Background: #e9e7df
- Border: 1px solid rgba(15,14,12,0.12)
- Border-radius: 8px
- Padding: 32px

Navigation:
- Fixed, full width, transparent on load
- On scroll: frosted glass pill, backdrop-filter blur(18px)
- Logo left, nav links center, CTA right

## Logo
Wordmark: "eco" lowercase Inter Semibold
The "o" is replaced by a segmented ring with × at centre
One segment in forest green (#1a4d2e), rest in near-black (#0f0e0c)
"studio" sits above "eco" in Inter Light, smaller size, muted color (#5b5751)

## Mood
Warm, editorial, considered, premium but human
Generous spacing, low density, structural whitespace
Lagos-rooted, globally credible
